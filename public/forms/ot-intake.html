⚠ Ava's Hub Attendance Policy — Please Read Carefully
A $90 fee applies to all last-minute cancellations and no-shows. This fee is the family's financial responsibility and is not billable to insurance.
A "last-minute cancellation" is any cancellation made with less than 24 hours' notice prior to the scheduled appointment.
After three (3) no-shows, the client will be discharged from active services and returned to the waitlist. Re-enrollment is not guaranteed.
Ava's Hub understands that emergencies occur. Please contact us as early as possible by phone or at hello@avashubnj.com whenever a cancellation is needed.
Consistent attendance is essential to your child's progress. We ask families to prioritize scheduled appointments to support the best possible outcomes.
