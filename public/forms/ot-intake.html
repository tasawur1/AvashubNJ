Occupation — Roles & Activities O
PEO Fit — How well does it all come together? PEO

The PEO model looks at how well the Person, their Environment, and their Occupations fit together. When the fit is poor, participation suffers. Rate each one for your child right now.

Person fit — How well do your child's current skills match what is expected of them day to day?
Environment fit — How well do your child's home, school, and community environments support their needs?
Occupation fit — How well do your child's daily activities and roles match their interests, values, and abilities?
